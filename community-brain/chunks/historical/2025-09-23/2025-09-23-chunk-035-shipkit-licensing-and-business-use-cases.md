# ShipKit licensing and business use cases

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-23

**Summary:** Brandon explained that ShipKit licensing is intended for personal or corporate use to build proprietary projects rather than for mass redistribution. The team concluded that while customers can freely adapt, modify, and sell derivative works, they are prohibited from sharing the original, unaltered templates with others.
