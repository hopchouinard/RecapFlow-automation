# Automating development administrative tasks

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-23

**Summary:** The participants discuss the risks of executive pressure to prematurely adopt "vibe coding" for complex enterprise tasks, highlighting the instability that comes from prioritizing rapid development over actual administrative requirements. They conclude that such shortcuts create long-term technical debt, emphasizing the necessity of maintaining realistic, safe, and disciplined development processes rather than settling for unverified, high-speed solutions.
