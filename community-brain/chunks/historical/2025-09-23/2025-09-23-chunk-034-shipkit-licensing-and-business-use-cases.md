# ShipKit licensing and business use cases

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-23

**Summary:** Brandon Hancock clarified that ShipKit is primarily designed for indie developers and freelancers—incorporating real-world expertise like the RAG template—rather than large-scale enterprise clients. They concluded that ShipKit is unsuitable for enterprise use cases, which typically require complex infrastructure like dedicated Kubernetes clusters rather than the tools (e.g., Supabase) provided in the template.
