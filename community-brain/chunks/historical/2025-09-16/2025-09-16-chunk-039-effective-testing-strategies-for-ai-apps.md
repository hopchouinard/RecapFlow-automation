# Effective Testing Strategies for AI Apps

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-16

**Summary:** To ensure AI app consistency and avoid context confusion, the group concluded that developers should generate a defined implementation plan from existing code and test that plan in fresh, separate chat sessions. This iterative approach forces the AI to critically re-evaluate the logic rather than relying on potentially cluttered conversation history.
