**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-16

**Summary:** Patrick and Brandon discussed how advanced AI workflows move beyond simple model interaction by utilizing "conversation managers" to handle context, distillation, and intent recognition. They concluded that effective implementation requires complex decision-making layers—such as model routers and system prompts—to determine the appropriate model, effort, and verbosity for each user interaction.
