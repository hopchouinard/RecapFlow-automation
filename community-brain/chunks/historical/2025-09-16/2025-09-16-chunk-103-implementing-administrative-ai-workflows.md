# Implementing Administrative AI Workflows

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-16

**Summary:** Brandon and Patrick discuss the importance of using AI prompting to build systems—similar to the principles in *The E-Myth*—that allow users to achieve complex outcomes without needing technical knowledge. They conclude that while AI makes generating software intent easy, the true value lies in creating simplified workflows that abstract away technical requirements like pull requests, branching, and merging.
