# Automating Accounting Workflows with AI

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-16

**Summary:** Ty Wells discusses transitioning from AI-driven guided tours for accounting software to fully automating workflows like accounts payable and receivable via email parsing. He concludes that by automating these routine tasks directly, he has shifted the human role to primarily managing necessary decision-making and "human-in-the-loop" oversight.
