# Training Corporate Teams on AI

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-02

**Summary:** Patrick Chouinard emphasizes that training a team of 600 developers on AI is primarily a challenge of change management, opting to start by using AI for repetitive, mundane tasks to build trust before advancing to complex coding workflows. By focusing on these low-stakes wins, the team aims to gradually overcome resistance and transition toward a fully AI-integrated development environment.
