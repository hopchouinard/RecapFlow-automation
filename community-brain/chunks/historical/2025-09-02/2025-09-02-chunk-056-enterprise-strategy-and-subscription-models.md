# Enterprise Strategy and Subscription Models

**Session:** Weekly Coaching Call | **Date:** 2025-09-02

**Summary:** Al Cole and Brandon Hancock discussed a strategy of initially offering free, helpful tools to SMBs overwhelmed by technology to build trust and market distribution.
