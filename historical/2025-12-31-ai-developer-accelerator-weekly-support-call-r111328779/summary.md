## Topics

### AI Dev Tools & Workflows

  - [**Claude Code:** The primary IDE, valued for its parallel tasks, plan mode, and task templates.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=268.0)
      - [**Model Choice:** Opus is preferred over Sonnet for faster, higher-quality results.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=214.0)
      - [**API Usage:** High usage is common; multiple plans ($100–$200/mo) are used to avoid hitting limits.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=1100.0)
  - [**Production App Models:**](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=1934.0)
      - [**Gemini 3 Flash:** Emerging as the top choice for production apps.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=1966.0)
          - [**Rationale:** Delivers GPT-4o quality at a lower cost, making it ideal for user-facing applications.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=1966.0)
      - [**Custom Models:** Used for niche tasks (e.g., OCR, PII detection), not general-purpose coding.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=305.0)
          - [**Example:** DeepGram's medical model for accurate speech-to-text transcription of specialized terminology.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=395.0)
  - [**ShipKit Enhancements:** Patrick is building a self-improving ShipKit agent.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=2393.0)
      - [**Process:** Analyzes roadmaps → reorders tasks → implements with a confidence check (must be \>95%) → updates task docs with decisions.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=2061.0)
      - [**Key Insight:** Task documents serve as the project's "working memory," enabling seamless restarts and full context recovery.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=2242.0)

### Business & Strategy

  - [**High-Value Projects:** Focus on automating repeatable tasks with clear monetary outcomes. If turning off your service would cause clients to lose money, you've found a high-value opportunity.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=1380.0)
  - [**Client Work:**](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=3004.0)
      - [**Positioning:** Sell results, not the development method.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=3147.0)
      - [**Compensation:** Get paid for work. Do not accept equity in lieu of payment, as this often leads to non-payment.](https://fathom.video/share/KTQnpe2waqaD21gegbAq1GAmFdntnMBE?tab=summary&timestamp=3420.0)
